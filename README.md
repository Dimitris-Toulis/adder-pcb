# Adder + Capacitors charge PCB

This is a pcb with two parts:
1) A half-adder operated with two push buttons
2) A demo of the effect of resistance in the charge time of capacitors

## BOM
- 2 Battery holders
- 2 10uF Capacitors
- 6 5mm LEDs
- 12 NPN transistors
- 6 220Ω Resistors
- 8 4.7kΩ Resistors
- 5 47kΩ Resistors
- 3 Push buttons
